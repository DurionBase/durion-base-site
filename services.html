Our Services

Software Assurance

We provide stage‑appropriate security reviews across applications, blockchains, cryptography and AI/ML. Our process includes technical onboarding, regular touchpoints and a thorough read‑out of severity‑rated findings, maturity metrics and actionable guidance.

  • Application & smart contract audits
  • Cryptography & protocol analysis
  • AI/ML model robustness evaluations
  • Multi‑disciplinary security reviews

Security Engineering

Our engineers build secure systems and tools, supporting your development in languages such as Rust, C++, Python, Solidity and embedded firmware. We address open‑source supply chain risks, design and review APIs, integrate static and dynamic analysis, and harden infrastructure.

  • Secure architecture & code design
  • Dependency & supply chain auditing
  • Fuzzing, penetration testing & remediation
  • DevOps and CI/CD security integration

Research & Development

We conduct advanced research to discover vulnerabilities and emerging threats across blockchain ecosystems and traditional software. Our team builds and maintains open‑source tools that support the security community.

  • Design & invariant analysis for protocols
  • Fuzzing harnesses & invariant testing
  • Tool development (static analysis, fuzzers, monitors)
  • Cross‑chain & DeFi security studies

Training & Education

Empower your team with hands‑on workshops and tailored courses on secure coding, blockchain development, threat modelling and incident response. We equip developers, auditors and executives with the skills they need to succeed in high‑risk environments.

  • Threat modelling & secure design workshops
  • Smart contract & dApp development courses
  • Incident response & crisis communication training
  • Custom programs for developers & leadership
