Why Choose Durion Base?

We go beyond basic code reviews—our security process begins with a deep design review, defining key properties that must always hold. Using formal verification and fuzzing tools, we mathematically prove or disprove these properties, eliminating entire classes of vulnerabilities before they reach production. This rigorous approach gives you confidence that your systems are secure, robust and compliant.

Our team of experts brings together decades of software, cryptography, and cybersecurity experience to deliver real results. When an incident occurs, we’re on call 24/7 to respond and help you recover.
