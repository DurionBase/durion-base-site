About Durion Base

Durion Base is a Dubai‑headquartered IT consulting and blockchain security firm dedicated to enabling organisations to thrive in a digital‑first world. We bridge deep security research with practical engineering to deliver lasting improvements for our clients.

Our Story

Founded by engineers and security researchers, Durion Base set out to raise the bar for secure software. Over the years we’ve worked with startups and enterprises alike to design high‑assurance systems and navigate the complexities of modern cyber threats. Our team draws experience from finance, embedded systems and open‑source development.

Why Choose Us?

  • Deep expertise: We apply formal methods and advanced tooling to uncover vulnerabilities across multiple domains.
  • Attacker’s perspective: Our approach anticipates real‑world threats to deliver practical, actionable guidance.
  • Collaborative process: We integrate with your team through technical onboarding, open communication and thorough debriefs.
  • Open source commitment: We contribute to the community by releasing tools and research for the benefit of all.

Our People

Durion Base is home to engineers, cryptographers, researchers and strategists from around the globe. Together we share a passion for advancing the security of digital ecosystems and mentoring the next generation of builders.
