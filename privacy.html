Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when you get in touch with us. This may include your name, email address, phone number and company name. We may also gather non‑identifying data, such as your IP address and browser type, to understand how visitors use our website and improve its performance.

How We Use Information

Durion Base uses the information you provide solely to respond to your inquiries, deliver services and maintain our business relationship. We do not sell, rent or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. Usage data helps us analyse trends and administer our site.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience and collect usage statistics. You can configure your browser settings to disable cookies; however, some features of the site may not function properly without them.

Data Security

We employ appropriate technical and organisational measures to safeguard your personal information against unauthorised access, disclosure or destruction. While we strive to protect your data, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Third‑Party Links

Our website may contain links to third‑party sites or services. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third‑party sites you visit.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of or deletion of your personal information. If you have any questions or concerns about how we handle your data, please contact us using the details on our Contact page.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the revised policy on this page with an updated “Last updated” date. Your continued use of our site after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please get in touch via the contact methods listed on our Contact page.
